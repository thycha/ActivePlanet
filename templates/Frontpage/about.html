About Us

Welcome to Active Planet, India's first active play park, where adventure knows no bounds. Nestled amidst a 10,000 square feet vertical garden, this play park offers visitors of all ages a world of unforgettable experiences. With an exciting array of over 40 unique experiences and attractions, we cater to diverse tastes. Beyond the exhilarating activities, special events and celebrations further enhance the thrill for our visitors. Join us and explore the limitless space of adventure and joy.
